# Syllabus Mapping by Grade

## Grade 7: Cambridge Lower Secondary 0893 Stage 7 (Physics & Earth Focus)
Allocation: 3 hrs/week
Units covered:
    - Unit 7.3: Forces in Space (Gravity, Solar System, Orbits, Eclipses)
    - Unit 7.6: Energy and Sound (Energy transfers, dissipation, sound waves, vacuum)
    - Unit 7.7: Environment and Ecosystems (Plate tectonics, water cycle, atmosphere)
    - Unit 7.9: Electricity (Electron flow, conductors/insulators, series circuits)
Cognitive focus: Analogies, visual models, curiosity-building
Derivation: None (conceptual only)

## Grade 8: Cambridge Lower Secondary 0893 Stage 8/9 (Physics & Earth Focus)
Allocation: 3 hrs/week
Units covered:
    - Unit 8.7: Speed, Motion and Forces (speed=d/t, d-t graphs, balanced forces, moments)
    - Unit 8.4: Light and Colour (reflection, refraction, dispersion, colour addition)
    - Unit 8.8: Earth and Solar System (magnetic field, ecosystems, climate, asteroids)
    - Unit 8.9: Applications of Science (renewable energy, electromagnets, endo/exothermic)
    - Stage 9 intro: Pressure, Turning Forces, Magnetism
Cognitive focus: Graph interpretation, variables, fair testing
Derivation: Basic algebra (speed, moment, pressure)

## Grade 9: Half IGCSE 0625
Allocation: 3 hrs/week
Topics covered (first half of 0625):
    - Topic 1: Motion, Forces and Energy (1.1-1.8)
    - Topic 3: Waves (3.1-3.4)
    - Topic 4 (partial): Electricity and Magnetism (4.1-4.3)
Cognitive focus: CER structure, IGCSE command words (Calculate, Describe, Explain)
Derivation: SUVAT from v-t graph area, E_k from equations of motion

## Grade 10: Full IGCSE 0625 (Exam Year)
Allocation: 3 hrs/week
Topics covered:
    - Topic 2: Thermal Physics (completing 0625)
    - Topic 4 (rest): Electricity and Magnetism
    - Topic 5: Nuclear Physics
    - Topic 6: Space Physics
    - Full Past Paper drill (Paper 1, 2, 4, 5/6)
Cognitive focus: Speed, accuracy, exam technique, AO1/AO2/AO3 balance
Derivation: All IGCSE derivations mastered

## Grade 11: Cambridge AS Level 9702 (Topics 1-11)
Allocation: 4 hrs/week
Topics:
    1. Physical quantities and units (SI units, errors, scalars/vectors)
    2. Kinematics (SUVAT derivation, graphs)
    3. Dynamics (Newton's laws, momentum)
    4. Forces, density and pressure (moments, equilibrium, hydrostatic)
    5. Work, energy and power
    6. Deformation of solids (Hooke's law, Young modulus)
    7. Waves (progressive, Doppler, polarization)
    8. Superposition (stationary waves, diffraction, interference, gratings)
    9. Electricity (current, p.d., resistance, resistivity)
    10. D.C. circuits (Kirchhoff, potential dividers)
    11. Particle physics (atoms, nuclei, fundamental particles)
    + Paper 3 (Advanced Practical Skills) prep
    + Paper 5 (Planning, Analysis, Evaluation) intro
Cognitive focus: Deep derivations, error analysis, practical skills
Derivation: All AS derivations using geometry/algebra

## Grade 12: Cambridge A Level 9702 (Topics 12-25) + TKA Fisika
Allocation: 4 hrs/week
Cambridge A2 Topics:
    12. Motion in a circle
    13. Gravitational fields
    14. Temperature
    15. Ideal gases
    16. Thermodynamics
    17. Oscillations
    18. Electric fields
    19. Capacitance
    20. Magnetic fields
    21. Alternating currents
    22. Quantum physics
    23. Nuclear physics
    24. Medical physics
    25. Astronomy and cosmology
    + Paper 4 (A2 Structured Questions) drill
    + Paper 5 intensive
TKA Fisika Topics (integrated):
    - Kinematika (GLB, GLBB, Parabola, Melingkar)
    - Dinamika (Newton, Momentum, Impuls, Rotasi)
    - Fluida (Statis, Dinamis, Pascal, Archimedes, Bernoulli)
    - Gelombang (Bunyi, Cahaya, Optik)
    - Kalor & Termodinamika (Gas Ideal, Hukum Termodinamika, Mesin Kalor)
    - Kelistrikan (Listrik Statis, Rangkaian DC, Hukum Kirchhoff)
Cognitive focus: HOTS, iris kurikulum, USP prep
Derivation: All A2 derivations + TKA-specific (Bernoulli, Carnot, etc.)

## Weekly Sequence (Example for Grade 12 Semester 1)
Week 1-2: Review AS Level + TKA Kinematika
Week 3-6: Topic 12-13 (Circular + Gravity) + TKA Fluida
Week 7-9: Topic 14-15 (Temp + Ideal Gas) + TKA Kalor
Week 10-13: Topic 16-17 (Thermo + Oscillations) + TKA Termodinamika
Week 14-17: Topic 18-19 (Electric Fields + Capacitance) + TKA Listrik Statis
Week 18-19: Topic 20 (Magnetic Fields) - Post TKA Exam
Week 20-22: Topic 21-23 (AC, Quantum, Nuclear)
